what is the "matrix network"?

im probably not the best person to ask, so go check out the official organisation behind the spec buttttt

it's an open and decentralised protocol that allows for anyone to talk to anyone using whatever server they choose! think email but for real time chatting. so, i could be `@paradox:1024cats.social` (matrix uses colons instead of @s in the domain portion) and speak to `@alice:matrix.org`. our accounts are stored on different servers but we can speak just fine!

since its not owned by one entity, theres not much of a chance for corporate greed (ew) to take hold, and even if it does, you can always move to another server!
